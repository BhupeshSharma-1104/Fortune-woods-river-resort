About Our Resort

Laguna River Resort was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where the untamed beauty of nature exists in perfect harmony with sophisticated comfort. We offer a retreat designed for those who seek both adventure and tranquility.

Our luxury rooms and suites are designed to frame the stunning natural world. With private balconies, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a serene palette, the vibrant forest and flowing river become a living part of your stay.

Dining is a cornerstone of the Laguna experience. Our restaurant celebrates the region's bounty with innovative, farm-to-table cuisine, all served in an elegant setting overlooking the picturesque river and canopy

Discover the magic where the forest meets the river, and luxury meets the wild.
