Premium River View Cottage - Dandeli

₹4000 per person per night

Wake to the gentle sounds of the Kali River flowing just beyond your private balcony in our Premium River View Cottage. This handcrafted eco-retreat blends rustic charm with modern comforts, featuring locally-sourced teak furnishings, solar-powered climate control, and floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the ever-changing river scenery.

After days filled with adventure, unwind in your queen-sized bed with organic cotton linens, or relax on the private sit-out with a cup of locally-grown coffee as kingfishers dart across the water. The cottage's open design brings the outside in, with natural ventilation and carefully positioned windows that catch the river breezes.

Maharaja Tents - Luxury Camping Dandeli

₹3,500 per person per night

Experience royal camping in our luxurious Maharaja Tents, blending traditional charm with modern comforts. These spacious canvas tents are built on permanent platforms with hardwood floors, offering a glamorous take on the classic safari experience.

Each tent features four-poster beds draped in mosquito netting, private sit-out areas, and en-suite bathrooms with hot water. The tents are strategically placed to offer privacy while still providing beautiful forest views. At night, lanterns cast a warm glow over the luxurious interiors, creating a magical atmosphere.

Dormitory - Group Accommodation Dandeli

₹2,750 per person per night 8-12 Guests

Our spacious AC dormitory is perfect for groups, backpackers, and adventure seekers looking for comfortable yet affordable accommodation. The dorm features sturdy bunk beds with privacy curtains, individual reading lights, and charging points for each guest.

The shared space includes a common area with comfortable seating, a dining table, and a small kitchenette. Two attached bathrooms with hot water showers ensure minimal wait times. Lockers are provided for securing valuables, and the dorm opens onto a shared veranda with hammocks and seating.
