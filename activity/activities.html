River Rafting

Most Popular

Challenge the thrilling rapids of Kali River with our expert-guided rafting expeditions. Navigate through gushing whitewaters surrounded by lush forests, with opportunities to spot riverbank wildlife.

Short Rafting

  • Duration: 1-1.5 hours
  • Distance: 1.5 km
  • Rapids: I
  • Best for: Beginners, families with teens

Medium Rafting

  • Duration: 2-2.5 hours
  • Distance: 5.5 km
  • Rapids: I-III
  • Best for: Adventure seekers, groups

Long Rafting

  • Duration: 4-5 hours
  • Distance: 9.5 km
  • Rapids: I-VII
  • Best for: Experienced rafters, thrill-seekers

Additional Information:

  • All safety equipment provided (helmet, life jacket, paddle)
  • Professional river guides with each raft
  • Minimum age: 12 years (for short/medium), 16 years (for long)
  • Includes refreshments at midpoint (for medium/long trips)

Jungle Safari

A fun-filled journey through lush green forests where every turn reveals a new surprise and hidden beauty. Along the way, you might spot vibrant birds, playful monkeys, and other fascinating wildlife in their natural habitat.

  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Timings: Morning (6-9am) or Evening (3-6pm)
  • Group size: 6-8 people per jeep
  • Best for: All ages, wildlife enthusiasts

Additional Information:

  • Open jeep with experienced naturalist guide
  • Binoculars provided for better wildlife viewing
  • Chances to spot: deer, wild boar, langurs, peacocks, various birds
  • Morning safari better for bird watching
  • Evening safari better for mammal sightings

Boating

Glide along the gentle currents of Kali River in our traditional wooden boats, surrounded by lush forests and tranquil waters. Perfect for birdwatching and sunset views, this peaceful journey offers a unique perspective of Dandeli's natural beauty.

  • Duration: 1-1.5 hours
  • Timings: Throughout the day (best at sunrise/sunset)
  • Capacity: 4-6 people per boat
  • Best for: Relaxation, photography, bird watching

Additional Information:

  • Traditional coracle or wooden boats available
  • Experienced boatman who knows the river well
  • Great for spotting kingfishers, herons, and other water birds
  • Private boat booking available at extra cost

Guided Jungle Treks

Navigate Dandeli's dense forests with our experienced trackers, who'll help you identify animal trails and rare plant species. Enjoy safe, well-marked routes suitable for all ages, with chances to spot langurs, peacocks, and wild boars.

Forest Trek

  • Duration: 1.5-2 hours
  • Distance: 5-6 km
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best for: Nature enthusiasts

Additional Information:

  • Professional naturalist guide accompanies all treks
  • Water and light snacks provided on longer treks
  • Recommended to wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Early morning treks have highest wildlife sightings
  • Private group treks available

Kayaking

New

Paddle through Kali River's pristine waters on an exhilarating kayaking adventure, navigating gentle rapids and calm stretches surrounded by dense forests. Perfect for spotting riverbank wildlife while enjoying an active connection with nature.

Standard Adventure

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Distance: 5-6 km
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best for: Active travelers

Additional Information:

  • Single and double kayaks available
  • Includes basic paddling instruction
  • Safety kayaker accompanies all groups
  • Minimum age: 12 years
  • Best time: Early morning for calm waters

Zipline Adventure

Thrill Seeker

Soar over lush treetops on our 300-meter zipline across the Kali River valley. Feel the rush as you glide with panoramic forest views, safely harnessed with professional equipment.

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes (including safety briefing)
  • Length: 300 meters
  • Height: 50 meters above valley
  • Best for: Adrenaline lovers aged 12+

Additional Information:

  • Full safety harness and helmet provided
  • Professional guides assist with equipment
  • Weight limit: 30kg to 120kg
  • Includes 1 ride (additional rides at discount)
  • Photos available for purchase

Zorbing

Experience the thrill of rolling down our gentle slopes in a giant inflatable zorb ball, with refreshing splashes from the river. Perfect for adrenaline seekers and fun-loving groups looking for a unique watersport adventure.

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes per session
  • Rides: 3 rolls per session
  • Capacity: 1-2 people per zorb
  • Best for: Groups, fun-seekers

Additional Information:

  • Dry and wet zorbing options available
  • Safety briefing before each session
  • Minimum age: 8 years
  • Weight limit: 100kg per zorb
  • Group discounts available
