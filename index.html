Expert-Crafted Adventures

"From gentle floats to heart-pounding drops"

Our certified guides design experiences matching every skill level - gentle bamboo rafting for first-timers, Class III rapids for thrill-seekers, and twilight kayak tours to spot nocturnal wildlife. All equipment is professionally maintained, with safety briefings that turn novices into confident explorers. Explore Dandeli & Kali River Region

Located in the heart of one of Karnataka's most beautiful regions, Fortune Woods River Resort offers convenient access to these popular attractions:

Nagoda Backwaters

The Nagoda Backwaters are formed by the Nagoda Dam, a colossal structure that spans the Kali River. As you approach this tranquil paradise, you‚Äôll be immediately struck by the mesmerizing beauty that unfolds before your eyes.

Kavala Caves

Limestone caves housing a naturally formed Shivalinga, accessible via a trek through dense forest and over 300 steps; a popular spot for both spiritual seekers and adventure enthusiasts.

Ulavi Temple

A historic temple located 25km away, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage.

Sathodi Falls

Beautiful waterfall approximately 30km from the resort, perfect for day excursions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Dandeli for river rafting?

The best time for river rafting in Dandeli is from October to May when the water levels are ideal for both beginners and experienced rafters.

How far is Fortune Woods River Resort from Dandeli town?

We're located just 6km from Dandeli Bus Stand, approximately a 15-minute drive from the town center.

Do you offer packages for adventure activities?

Yes, we offer customized packages that include accommodation, meals, and adventure activities like rafting, kayaking, and jungle safaris.

Is the resort suitable for family vacations?

Absolutely! We have family-friendly accommodations and activities suitable for all age groups, including special programs for children.
